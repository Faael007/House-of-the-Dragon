Balerion

Balerion, called the Black Dread, was a dragon of House Targaryen. He was ridden by King Aegon I Targaryen during the War of Conquest.

Syrax

Syrax was a female dragon. She was ridden exclusively by Rhaenyra Targaryen. She was named after a Valyrian goddess.

Arrax

Arrax was a dragon ridden by Prince Lucerys Velaryon during the Dance of the Dragons.

Caraxes

Caraxes, also called the Blood Wyrm and Blood Worm, was the dragon ridden by Prince Aemon Targaryen during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and later by Prince Daemon Targaryen.

Seasmoke

Seasmoke was a light gray dragon. He was large enough to fight during the dance, but still a young dragon, and more agile in the air than his older brothers.

Vermax

Vermax was the dragon ridden by Prince Jacaerys Velaryon. He prospered and grew larger each year. The dragon became ill-tempered when near snow, ice, and cold. Vermax's color is not described in the books.

Vhagar

Vhagar was a female dragon ridden by Visenya during the Conquest, alongside Aegon the Conqueror's Balerion and her sister Rhaenys's Meraxes.
